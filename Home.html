Bio

I thought I was going to be a Chiropractor. One day my father sat me down and convinced me that I should try the family car business. That was over 35 years ago! I love sports, specifically I’m a passionate mountain biker, marathoner and a Padi scuba diving instructor. Being with nature is food for my soul. I look foreword to my annual canoe trip to Algonquin Park with my wife Jacquie and if I’m lucky, my 3 daughters as well.
